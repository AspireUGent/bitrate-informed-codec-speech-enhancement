Audio samples

This page presents audio samples for our submission Bitrate-Informed Coded Speech Enhancement.

The coded speech samples, and its enhanced speech samples by the proposed bitrate-informed coded speech enhancement model for LC3plus and AMR-WB codecs are shown here. Their corresponding clean speech samples before encoding are provided for comparison as well. Speech samples for LC3plus and AMR-WB codec include 5 bitrate and 8 bitrate speech samples, respectively, which are the same as experiments in our submission. There are 3 samples for each bitrate of each codec.

All experiment parameters are as described in our submission.
